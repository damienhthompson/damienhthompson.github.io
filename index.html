Technology Experiments & Decision Systems

I build small experimental systems to explore how emerging technologies — particularly AI, automation and data pipelines — change how organisations make decisions and operate.

These projects are not products. They are thinking tools — built to test ideas, understand the practical behaviour of modern platforms, and explore what becomes possible when AI is treated as infrastructure rather than a feature.

Decision System 📡
Radar — Executive Opportunity Evaluation Engine

A decision system that analyses job opportunities across two dimensions: role fit and career trajectory. Uses AI extraction pipelines and deterministic scoring models to generate a full application strategy.

Explores

  • AI document extraction & structured role analysis
  • Deterministic scoring with hard-rule overrides
  • Career trajectory modelling & sector-aware classification
  • Agentic pipelines with quality gates and auto-rewrite
