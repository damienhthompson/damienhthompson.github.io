Radar

Personal executive career intelligence platform. Transforms an executive technology leadership search from reactive job applications into a proactive, relationship-driven opportunity engine.

Pipeline — 8-node AI scoring engine evaluates every role across fit and career trajectory. Generates an application brief with lead angle, proof points, and positioning strategy.
Targets — Identifies organisations at technology inflection points before they hire. Scores against a 5-trigger model, discovers named executives via web search, generates tailored outreach by contact role.
Executive Network — Relationship management for recruiters, executives, and advisors. Interaction timelines, follow-up reminders, market intelligence capture.
